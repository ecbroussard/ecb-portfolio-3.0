About Elaine

Elaine Broussard is a digital design and communications professional from New Orleans, Louisiana. In her current role as Desktop Publisher/Assistant Editor at MasteryPrep, a test prep startup, Elaine has proven herself as one of the most versatile members of the company’s product development team. From writing and editing a reading passage one moment, to designing an interior book template the next, to coding a marketing website the next, Elaine demonstrates enthusiasm and professionalism in every task she completes.

Previously Elaine worked as a newspaper reporter, copy editor and a high school English teacher. She has earned bachelor's degrees in journalism and digital design and a master's degree in English education. She is currently pursuing a second master's degree in mass communications and web design from the University of Florida.

In her private life, she divides her free time between the Baton Rouge and New Orleans areas. Her favorite recreational activities include listening to audiobooks, scouring the Internet for unique recipes to try at home, singing karaoke, playing board games and debating politics.
