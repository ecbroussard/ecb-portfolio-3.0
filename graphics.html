Graphic Design

I've done a significant amount of work in the realm of graphic design. At my current job I am the lead desktop publisher, tasked with interior book template design and print publication layout. I've also dabbled a bit in brand scheme and logo design, digital illustration, and marketing email design. Please see my work samples below.

Raiders' Digest Redesign

As the moderator of the Raiders' Digest at Archbishop Rummel High School, I could choose between laying out the school newspaper myself or allowing the printing company to do it for me. The previous moderator simply sent the stories and photos to the printing company, and a member of the company's staff completed the layout. After seeing the previous design, I knew I needed to give it an overhaul. It was extra work on my part, but I am pleased with my redesign. Please view the before and after images below.
